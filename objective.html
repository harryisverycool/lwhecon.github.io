Objectives

Students should be trained to do the following:

  • To foster an aesthetic sense and to stimulate ideas of creativity.
  • To establish a positive healthy eating attitude by understanding the nutritive value and functions of different food groups.
  • To acquire knowledge and to develop the manipulative skills required for cooking.
  • To understand the importance of personal hygiene and food hygiene in daily life.
  • To implement the four generic skills: collaboration, communication, creativity and critical thinking.
  • Be aware of current issues on health and to develop a positive attitude towards health and fitness.
